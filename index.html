Instructions

  • Move pawn with the black arrows above (on touchscreen) or arrow keys (on keyboard).
  • You can only go through doors when your pawn is the color of the door.
  • In world: Move to a level number to start. Use the buttons to Save your game or Quit. If you get stuck, Save and Quit and load your saved game... or just enter and exit any level.
  • In level: Get to the goal rainbow after collecting all coins. If you get stuck & want to start the level over, click/tap Restart or press R. Use the To World button to exit the level.
  • Your color:
    1. You begin in a neutral/colorless state.
    2. You will take on the color of the first colored ring you touch.
    3. After that, when you touch rings, your color will change by 50-50 blending.
    4. (Hint: that means that the order in which you touch rings can be significant.)
  • Some levels have subtle color variations; high screen brightness is recommended.
  • If you want to use Jump to Level, you must know the level (not world) number and title.
